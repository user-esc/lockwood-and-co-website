Lockwood & Co.

An independent psychical investigation agency based in London, England, founded by Anthony Lockwood. Despite being the city's smallest agency, our team of sensitives are more than capable of handling any haunting!

Our Mission

Here at Lockwood & Co., we are dedicated to serving the public in Britain's fight against the Problem. We investigate hauntings, dealing with dangerous manifestations, with the goal of keeping the public safe. Whether you're troubled by lurkers, cold maidens, or wraiths, we're here to handle any disturbances.

1. Locate the visitor

Utilize our talents to identify a visitor and track its vanishing point to locate the source.

2. Contain the source

Retrieve and seal the source with protective materials to eventually be disposed of.
