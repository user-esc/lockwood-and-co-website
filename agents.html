Meet the Team!

Don't underestimate the capabilities of our operatives!

We possess extensive experience and capabilities to effectively operate without any adult supervisors. While unconventional, this unique agency structure provides our team with greater freedom within investigative operations, minimizing interferences and unnecessary oversight.

DEPRAC Certified!

Our agents have all obtained their Grade 4 certificates from the Department of Psychical Research and Control (DEPRAC), so we are fully qualified to handle dangerous hauntings.

Anthony Lockwood

Leader/Founder

Previously trained under, Nigel "Gravedigger" Sykes, Lockwood is highly skilled in rapier combat and ghost fighting. He leverages his ability of sight to detect ghosts, death-glows, and residual energies, taking a quick, yet thorough approach to hauntings. Lockwood is not one to back down from a challenge and is always eager to solve a case!

Lucy Carlyle

Junior Field Operative

A sharp-witted and driven sensitive possessing high-level field expertise, with her strongest talents residing in listening and touch. Lucy has been working for agencies since the age of 8, honing her abilities to become one of London's most proficient Listeners. Her approach to investigations is guided by empathy and a unique connection with spirits.

George Cubbins

Deputy

Alongside his role of deputy, George is the team's primary researcher, proficient in data collection and analysis, taking a fastidious and careful approach to investigations. He previously worked for the Fittes institute as an agent and top-notch researcher. Outside of this role, George is passionate about cooking and always excited to experiment with new recipes!

After dark, ring the bell and wait beyond the iron line.
